    IIITDM Kurnool


    FOUNDATION

    The Indian Institute of Information technology Design and Manufacturing, Kurnool is one of the centrally funded IIITs proposed by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. IIITDM Kurnool launched its academic program with the support of its mentor Institute, IIITDM Kancheepuram (Tamil Nadu), on August 2015 from the Kancheepuram campus.

    STUDENT LIFE

    The students have started several clubs for developing the skills and are learning a lot of technical and soft- skills from their seniors. All the students participate in the Technical and cultural activities. Solasta the annual Techno cultural fest, Open day, are some activities in which the students are deeply involved and fine-tunning their skills.

    Computer Science And Engineering

    The Institute is offering B.tech and PH.D. Courses at present in this department and is starting to take admission for M.tech from 2020 with specialization in Data Analytics and Decision Sciences

    Electrical And Communication Engineering

    B.tech and PH.D. Courses are offered in this department, the Institute is starting to take admission for M.tech from 2020 with Electronic System Design under this department

    Mechanical Technology

    B.tech and PH.D. Courses are offered in this department, the Institute is starting to take admission for M.tech from 2020 with Smart Manufacturing under this department
